For kids

Are you a young person looking to learn more about science? Come on down to our museum, there's plenty to see and do. You can learn about Newtonian physics from our bumper swing, or why not travel back in time and meet our resident Woolly Mammoth?

For teachers

We want to work with schools to create places of learning and exploring. we will give your students guided tours of the museum and teach them in our learning laboratory. Please get in touch to arrange a time to bring your students through for a visit.

Researchers

Are you looking to get involved with our team of researchers and academics? Our museum offers various ways for you to use our resources and contribute towards them. We have online records, laboratory space, and a working relationship with a number of universities around the country.
