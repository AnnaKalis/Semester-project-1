Location

Museum is located at Solemskogveien 33, Oslo. Conveniently situated, it can be easily accessed by bike, car, and public transport.

By bike: Enjoy a ride through Oslo's streets and parks to reach the museum. There are designated bike lanes for a safe and pleasant journey.

By car: If driving, simply enter "Solemskogveien 33" into your GPS or navigation app. Parking spaces are available for visitors.

By public transport: The museum's location is just a short walk from the nearest bus stop.

Admission

The admision is free to all visitors, ensuring that everyone can explore the wonders within our walls without any financial barriers.

We offer guidet tours where our expert guides will lead you through the captivating exhibitions, sharing fascinating stories and insights behind the masterpieces and artifacts on display.

If you would like to organise a guided tour for your group of 6 or more people, please contact us to arrange the tour.

Accessibility

Accessibility is at the core of our mission, and we have worked tirelessly to ensure that our museum is an inclusive space for all.

Our building is equipped with ramps and elevators to accommodate visitors with mobility challenges, and we provide sign language interpretation for those who are deaf or hard of hearing.

Additionally, we offer tactile tours and descriptive audio guides for visually impaired visitors.
